About Our Farm

Located on 150 acres of rolling countryside in northern Carroll County, MD, the Colonial Heritage Farm endeavors to provide healthy, sustainably raised meat and eggs, while preserving colonial-era farming history.

We offer grass-fed beef, lamb, and eggs from pasture-raised heritage breeds including Scottish Highland Cattle, Karukul Sheep, and Dominique Chickens.

We also offer English Shepherd puppies for sale throughout the year.
