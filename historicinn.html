Colonial Inn

The Colonial Inn is a charming boutique Inn located on a historic farm dating back to the colonial period.

Enjoy the peace, solitude and beauty of this historic country setting. It is the perfect setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.

The Inn's special features include a beautiful historic stone main house, stone smoke house, cottage house and historic barns, all of which have been preserved.

The Inn features a large private suite in the main stone house and an entire suite in the cottage house. The Inn is situated on a 170-acre working Scottish Highland cattle farm.
