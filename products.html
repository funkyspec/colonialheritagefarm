Healthier Beef

Colonial Heritage Beef is quintessentially healthier and more nutritious. As important, the beef is flavorful and delicious. The beef is 100% natural, grass-fed and pasture raised. As a result of the Scottish Highland breed’s thick coat of long hair, the beef is naturally lean.

Furthermore, Scottish Highland beef is generally considered more of a premium grass-fed beef since it is well-marbled and rich in flavor.

Scottish Highland beef has old fashioned beef qualities that ensure tenderness and succulence with a very distinctive flavor.

Scottish Highland cattle are actually the ancient, Grande Olde breed preferred by the British Royal Family.

Colonial Heritage Beef has the following health and nutrition benefits:

  • Lean and 97% fat free
  • Fewer in calories
  • Lower in cholesterol
  • More protein
  • More beta-carotene
  • More omega-3 fatty acids
  • More conjugated linoleic acid (CLA)
  • A healthier ratio of omega-6 to omega-3 fatty acids
  • More vitamin E
  • Free of chemical pesticides
  • Free of Antibiotics
  • Free of artificial growth hormones
  • Free of MSG
  • Free of animal byproducts
  • Free of gluten
