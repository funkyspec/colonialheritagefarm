Why Highland Craft Beef is Healthier

Colonial Farms is laser focused on producing and marketing a unique type of Craft Beef referred to as "Highland Craft Beef."

Highland Craft Beef is healthier and more nutritious for consumers. The beef is also flavorful and delicious. As a result of the Highland breed's thick coat of long hair, the beef is naturally lean. The beef is also higher in protein and other nutrients compared to factory beef.

Highland Craft Beef is generally considered more of a premium grass-fed beef since it marbles well and is rich in taste even when grass finished. Highland Craft Beef has old fashioned beef qualities that ensure tenderness and succulence with a very distinctive flavor.

Our Highland Craft Beef has the following health and nutrition benefits:

  • Leaner
  • Fewer in calories
  • Lower in cholesterol
  • More protein
  • More beta-carotene
  • More omega-3 fatty acids
  • More conjugated linoleic acid (CLA)
  • A healthier ratio of omega-6 to omega-3 fatty acids
  • More vitamin E
  • Free of chemical pesticides
  • Free of antibiotics
  • Free of artificial growth hormones
  • Free of MSG
  • Free of animal byproducts
  • Free of gluten

Highland Cattle are knows as the ancient "Grande Olde" breed and is the preferred beef of the British Royal Family.

Craft Beef Defined

Colonial Farms is part of the growing "Craft Beef" movement. The beef industry is currently undergoing a huge transformation. There is a significant resurgence of small family farms raising specialty beef breeds and creating unique tastes of beef for sale at local restaurants and at local farmers markets. This industry transformation is similar to the explosion that occurred in the markets for specialty cheeses and craft beers.

Generally speaking, Craft Beef is often described in the following manner:

  1. Craft Beef is a premium beef from a specialty breed of cattle, like a Heritage breed, that is not a cross breed. Heritage breeds are historic breeds that are typically smaller, slower growing and that have fallen out of favor in today's industrial farming business.
  2. Craft Beef is healthier and more nutritious because it is typically nutrient rich, higher in protein, lower in cholesterol, lower in calories, free of antibiotics and free of artificial growth hormones, among various other benefits.
  3. Craft Beef is raised using sustainable agricultural practices and is 100% natural, pasture raised and grass-fed.
  4. Craft Beef is raised on family farms in small batches, not on large industrial farms.
  5. Craft Beef is raised humanely, under less stressful conditions. Less stressful beef tastes better and is more tender.
