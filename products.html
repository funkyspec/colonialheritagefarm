Our Beef

Our Beef

The Colonial Heritage Farm's history dates back to the American Revolution and the Declaration of Independence in 1776. In recognition of this history, the Colonial Heritage Farm markets an independent brand of traditional beef.

Colonial Heritage Beef is quintessentially old fashioned. It is best when smoked, cured and/or seasoned the old fashioned way, and prepared with natural curing agents and authentic seasonings.

Colonial Heritage Beef is a premium beef, which is lean, well-marbled and rich in flavor. It has traditional beef qualities that ensure tenderness and succulence with a very distinctive flavor.

Colonial Heritage beef is not only delicious, but it is healthy and nutritious. It does not typically utilize artificial nitrates and nitrites or artificial preservatives and colors or added phosphates. There has never been any use of antibiotics or growth hormones. The beef has no MSG and is 97% fat free and gluten free.

The Colonial Heritage Farm strives to market traditional beef as part of a dedication to authenticity and as a tribute to the patriotic “Spirit of 1776.”
