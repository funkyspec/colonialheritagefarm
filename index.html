Our farm

Located on 150 acres of rolling countryside in northern Carroll County, MD, the Colonial Heritage Farm endeavors to provide healthy, sustainably raised meat and eggs, while preserving colonial-era farming history. Learn more »

Our produce

We offer grass-fed beef, lamb, and eggs from pasture-raised heritage breeds including Scottish Highland Cattle, Karukul Sheep, and Dominique Chickens.

We also offer English Shepherd puppies for sale throughout the year. Learn more »

Our history

Established as a working farm in 1744 by Michael MacQuire and his wife Patience, our core farm property has been in almost continuous production ever since. Learn more »
