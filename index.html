The Colonial Heritage Farm is a 170 acre historic farm located in Westminster, Maryland that endeavors to preserve our agricultural heritage. The Farm dates back to the 1700s and the American Revolution and focuses on preserving our rich farming history. The Colonial Heritage Farm raises and markets heritage livestock breeds and utilizes sustainable farming practices.
