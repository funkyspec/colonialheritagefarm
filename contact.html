Internship Program

The Colonial Heritage Farm typically has internship positions open to prospective farmers.

The internship program is suited for people who have an interest in learning more about sustainable farming and raising and marketing heritage beef. You would be exposed to many aspects of farm operations, farm maintenance and agriculture product marketing and sales.

The internship program is from 4-12 months and designed to give interns an introduction to farming from a first hand perspective. Interns usually work 7 half days a week with afternoons open to explore the local area or work another part time job. Housing could be available as well.
